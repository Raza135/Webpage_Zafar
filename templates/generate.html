To generate a paragraph, enter Urdu keywords in the following boxes. You can provide up to five keywords but at least one. Model will try to generate a paragraph in context of given keywords. You can also provide feedback after paragraph has been generated. Feedback comprises of score on coherency, relevance to the keywords and grammar of generated paragraph.

Note that the following text-boxes for keywords have set to type in Urdu. You just need to type in English and the characters will be transcribed to their phonetic counterparts in Urdu.
