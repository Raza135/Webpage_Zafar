Zafar is a Deep Learning based Automated Paragraph generator for Urdu language

Let's generate paragraphs!

Project "Zafar: Deep Learning based Automated Topical Urdu Paragraph Generator" has been developed by "Team Ghalib", a team of four Habib University students, name: Muhammad Ahmed, Raza Naqvi, Kabir and Hamza Rizvi. Basic idea of project is to generate a paragraph of a story in Urdu upon given a few keywords. Goal is to generate a relevant, coherent, meaningful and grammatically correct paragraph.

Our work is first of its kind for Urdu language. For Urdu previously, work has been done in domain of plagiarism detection, summary generation and caption generation but we didn't find any substantial work for automatically generating paragraphs on a specified topic. We hope that our work will bring substantial contribution to Natural Language Generation (NLG), a subfield of NLP, for Urdu. We also hope that this work will serve as basis of further advanced research in the domain of automated language generation for Urdu.
